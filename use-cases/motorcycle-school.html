Eliminating £8 Mistakes in UK Motorcycle Training

How Kiwacht-Edge saves Motorcycle schools from voided certificates, manual errors, and wasted hours

The Pain Point

UK motorcycle training schools face a paper-based workflow:

  • The DVSA, UK's Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency, sells books of 25 paper certificates to Approved Training Bodies (ATBs)
  • After each successful CBT (compulsory basic training), the instructor fills out a certificate by hand
  • Staff then manually retype all 25 certs into Excel for DVSA submission
  • One typo = voided certificate = £8 loss
  • For a small school of three instructors 3 voids per 20 clients = £1200/year lost
  • Plus three hours of admin per weekend

One of the Quirky DVSA Rules

Duration must be entered in decimal hours by the instructor on the day, but not how you’d expect:

  • 1 hour = 1.00
  • 15 minutes = 0.25
  • 30 minutes = 0.50
  • 45 minutes = 0.75 and so on

Get this wrong? Certificate voided.

The Kiwacht-Edge Workflow

Instructor → Kiwacht-Edge PWA → On-prem hub → DVSA. No cloud. No data leakage.

  1. Instructor or admin opens Kiwacht-Edge PWA on any smartphone (iOS/Android)
  2. Enters the 8 DVSA fields:
    • ATB Number
    • Certificate Number (auto-incremented: 1–25)
    • Completion Time
    • Course Duration (e.g., “4h 12m”)
    • Driver Number (9-char DVLA ID)
    • Instructor Certificate
    • Restriction Duplicate
    • Transmission ATB Number
  3. Kiwacht-Edge applies DVSA rules:
    • Converts “4h 12m” → 4.25
    • Validates driver number format
    • Prevents duplicate cert numbers
  4. Data sent securely to school’s on-prem hub (a €200 PC in the office)
  5. Hub collates all 25 certs, generates DVSA-compliant CSV
  6. Owner reviews → approves → exports
  7. All data erased — ephemeral by design

Why This Matters for Europe

This isn’t just about one particular workflow issue of a motorcycle training school. It’s about fixing small workflows for 99% of European businesses:

  • ✅ Niche but real: ~500 ATBs, ~2,000 instructors in the UK alone
  • ✅ Regulated & high-stakes: DVSA compliance is non-negotiable
  • ✅ Sovereign by necessity: No cloud AI can handle paper-to-digital conversion with zero data leakage
  • ✅ Scalable pattern: Same architecture works for driving schools, HGV trainers, etc.

Outcome: 12 hours saved/month. £1,200/year recovered. Zero voids.
