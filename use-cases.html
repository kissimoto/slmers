Motorcycle School

Instructors complete paper DVSA certificates after each lesson. Staff manually transcribe 8 fields into Excel — including error-prone decimal conversions (e.g., 5 minutes = 0.08 hours). One typo voids the certificate, costing £8.

Kiwacht-Edge lets staff scan or type raw data → applies DVSA rules → auto-fills the spreadsheet with perfect formatting. Human approves before submission.

Result: Zero voids. Full compliance. Hours saved per week.
