What is an SLM?

A Small Language Model (SLM) is a lightweight AI, typically under 4 billion parameters — far smaller than OpenAI’s GPT (175B+).

On its own, an SLM can summarise, translate, or assist with code. But with custom platforms like Kiwacht-Edge, it powers real-world business workflows: voice alerts in retail, invoice validation for accountants, DVSA form automation, or license-compliant asset handling in creative agencies.

The future of AI isn’t bigger models, it’s smaller, faster, sovereign intelligence that runs offline, respects GDPR, and stays under your control.

SLMs use a fraction of the energy: they can run on a standard office PC, while LLMs require massive data centers.

Why Gmail’s AI Is a Watershed Moment

Google isn’t just adding a convenience feature.
It’s normalising the idea that your most sensitive business communications, client briefs, contracts, financial disputes, creative IP, should be processed by a US-based AI on foreign servers, as a default.

Even if Google claims it “doesn’t train on your data,” the act of sending every email to its cloud infrastructure creates three irreversible shifts:

  1. Data Flow Becomes Invisible
    Users don’t see where their data goes, it apparently just “works.” But under current US law (Executive Order 14086), US authorities can access data held by US companies, even if it’s from EU citizens. There’s no meaningful opt-out for sovereignty.
  2. “Free AI” = Data Extraction
    The reason it’s free is because your workflow becomes the training ground. Not necessarily for public models today, but for:
    • Behavioural patterns (“How do agencies respond to billing complaints?”)
    • Entity (important facts) extraction (“Which clients mention ‘billboards’?”)
    • Market intelligence (“Which SMEs are struggling with compliance?”)
    This is passive surveillance disguised as productivity.
  3. Sovereignty Becomes a Premium Feature
    If you don’t want your emails processed by US AI giants, you should go out of your way to find alternatives, if you don't your info will be extracted.
